unit 01

What is money,
and why money

01

medium of exchange

Money replaces the barter system — instead of trading goods directly, we use a universal token everyone accepts.

02

store of value

Money holds worth over time. You can earn it today and spend it months or years from now.

03

unit of account

Money gives us a consistent way to measure and compare the value of completely different goods and services.
